Keepers Sparkling Coffee

Keepers was a lot of things.

First and foremost it was a sparkling coffee brand, sourcing only the highest quality beans from the best independent farms.

We got people from around the world to re-think what coffee is and how it’s made and enjoyed.

Creating a memorable refreshing version of coffee you’ve never tasted before.

Keepers still is a community.

Our company started on the backs of friends and family who believed in us.

From helping us hand-bottle till 4 in the morning, to helping us sell at local markets.

We are a group of friends who love new ideas and making things happen for each other.

When you picked up a Keepers

Each can was a clear reflection of the soil, the farmer, the roaster, and the brewer.

Lastly, Keepers was Thi & Brent, just two very normal guys who care a lot about our coffee, our friends, and our work.

Thi has gone on to start Garnish Studios, creating content for food companies, and Brent has gone back to running Condensed, a branding agency for startups.

We like meeting creative people with big ideas, let’s do something together.
